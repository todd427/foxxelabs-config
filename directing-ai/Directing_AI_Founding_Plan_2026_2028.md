### <span style="color:#16A085">The founding plan, 2026–2028</span>

<span style="color:#7F8C8D">**Operational document — 11 May 2026** · Author: Todd McCaffrey</span>
<span style="color:#7F8C8D">**Status:** Working plan for founding-author phase of discipline development</span>

## <span style="color:#1B4F72">Working assumptions</span>

This plan rests on five working assumptions, each of which can be revisited as the work proceeds:

<span style="color:#34495E">**One.**</span> The dissertation completes on schedule (12 June 2026) and the viva is completed by August 2026. If either slips, the post-viva start date slips correspondingly.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Two.**</span> Miteo is a *Directing AI* example — a real research programme whose methodology is itself an application of the discipline. Miteo's substantive research outputs and the discipline's foundational artefacts are produced through the same work, not as competing demands on attention.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Three.**</span> A Research PhD at ATU is the institutional vehicle for the founding-author work. The PhD application is pursued in parallel with the discipline-building work from August 2026; once admitted, the discipline-building work proceeds under ATU's institutional auspices.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Four.**</span> ATU remains the primary institutional partner. Other institutional and professional-body conversations continue as parallel options rather than alternatives, with the operating assumption that ATU is the natural home unless something specific changes that.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Five.**</span> The window for founding-author work is roughly two years of full effort plus eighteen months of partial effort, bounded by competing demands on attention rather than by any other factor.

## <span style="color:#1B4F72">The three threads, merged</span>

The plan treats three previously-separate workstreams as a single unified thread:

**Miteo as substantive research.** A real mitochondrial-research programme producing publishable scientific outputs in a domain outside Todd's native expertise, using AI tools and deliberate methodology to compensate.

**Directing AI as discipline-building.** The authoring of foundational artefacts (pattern catalogue, case library, textbook, public website, convening events, opinion pieces) that establish *Directing AI* as a recognised academic discipline.

**Research PhD at ATU as institutional vehicle.** Doctoral candidacy that provides academic standing, institutional supervision, and a credentialed pathway for the foundational work to enter the academic record.

The plan's central premise is that these three threads can produce their outputs through the same activity. Weekly work on Miteo produces both mitochondrial research progress and case-library material. Pattern-catalogue entries derived from Miteo encounters become thesis chapter content. The convening event becomes both discipline-establishing and a PhD-relevant academic conference. The unifying activity is *the documented practice of directing AI on a real research programme by the discipline's founding author*.

## <span style="color:#1B4F72">The plan, by quarter</span>

### <span style="color:#16A085">Q3 2026 (July–September) — Foundations</span>

The first three months after the viva. The focus is on establishing the discipline's public location, opening the PhD application conversation at ATU, and beginning the documented Miteo work.

<span style="color:#34495E">**July 2026.**</span> Viva preparation and viva. No substantive work on this plan during the viva run-up; protect that time. Post-viva: brief decompression week.

<span style="color:#34495E">**August 2026.**</span>
- *Discipline-building.* Reserve the namespace (domain, organisation, citation infrastructure). Stand up a simple public website with the framework articulated, the six anchor cases at brief depth, the starter pattern catalogue, and a clear public identity for *Directing AI* as a discipline in formation. The site does not need to be polished; it needs to exist.
- *Research PhD.* Initial conversation with potential ATU supervisors. Likely candidates: the MSc supervisor; the cyberpsychology programme director; one academic in computing or applied AI. Aim: identify one primary and one co-supervisor by end of month. Topic framing: open at this stage, to be refined through supervisor conversations.
- *Miteo.* Resume the parked Miteo work. Begin a weekly research log explicitly framed as *directing AI on a real research programme* — fuzz readings, patterns applied, Borrowed Authority moments caught, decisions made. The log becomes both the case-library material and the future thesis appendix.

<span style="color:#34495E">**September 2026.**</span>
- *Discipline-building.* Pattern catalogue work begins systematically. Target: ten patterns drafted in Alexandrian template by end of month, drawing on the existing portfolio's named patterns and on the Miteo work to date.
- *Research PhD.* Research proposal drafted in collaboration with the identified supervisor(s). Topic likely scoped as something like *"Directing AI as a Methodological Discipline: Theoretical Framework and Empirical Application in Mitochondrial Research"* — using Miteo as the empirical vehicle for a methodological PhD. Formal application submitted to ATU graduate research office.
- *Miteo.* Continue research log. Begin drafting the first Miteo working paper, written from the start with deliberate *Directing AI* methodology applied and documented.

### <span style="color:#16A085">Q4 2026 (October–December) — Public Discipline</span>

The discipline acquires a public presence. The first foundational artefacts circulate publicly. The PhD application moves to decision.

<span style="color:#34495E">**October 2026.**</span>
- *Discipline-building.* Pattern catalogue v0.5 published openly (Creative Commons licence). Approximately fifteen patterns in Alexandrian template. The publication does not need to be complete; it needs to be public. Announcement post on the discipline's website and through Todd's existing networks.
- *Research PhD.* ATU decision on application. If accepted, confirmation of supervision and target start date (most likely January 2027 or autumn 2027).
- *Miteo.* First working paper in active draft. Methodological appendix being written alongside the substantive content.

<span style="color:#34495E">**November 2026.**</span>
- *Discipline-building.* Irish Times opinion piece submitted (or whichever venue lands the right audience). The piece introduces *Directing AI* as a discipline to a general professional readership, points at the public website, and signals that foundational work is happening at ATU through Todd's PhD candidacy.
- *Research PhD.* If accepted, begin formal preparation for PhD start. If not yet decided, follow up; if rejected, immediate consideration of alternative institutional routes (see *Decision Points* below).
- *Miteo.* First working paper draft complete. Begin internal review with available reviewers.

<span style="color:#34495E">**December 2026.**</span>
- *Discipline-building.* Pattern catalogue v0.5 → v0.8 expansion based on Miteo material and external feedback. Begin scoping the spring 2027 convening event: venue, date, target audience, format. Twenty practitioners over two days in Letterkenny or Dublin.
- *Research PhD.* Formal PhD start (if January start) or continued preparation (if autumn start). Initial supervisor meetings, year-one structured component planning, confirmation review timeline.
- *Miteo.* First working paper finalised. Decision on publication venue: pre-print server, methodologically-focused journal, or interdisciplinary venue. The paper is the first piece of academic-record evidence that *Directing AI* methods produce defensible scholarly work.

### <span style="color:#16A085">Q1 2027 (January–March) — Catalogue and Convening</span>

The first substantial foundational artefact lands. The discipline acquires its first community moment.

<span style="color:#34495E">**January 2027.**</span>
- *Discipline-building.* Pattern catalogue v1 published. Twenty-five patterns and twelve anti-patterns in Alexandrian template, with worked examples drawn from the six anchor cases and from Miteo. The catalogue is the first foundational artefact of the discipline in publishable form. Public announcement, press to relevant audiences, distribution through Todd's networks.
- *Research PhD.* Year-one work proceeds. Reading and structured component activity. Beginning of formal thesis chapter scoping — first chapters likely on the discipline's conceptual framework, drawing directly on the existing portfolio documents.
- *Miteo.* First working paper published as pre-print. Continued research progress on the substantive mitochondrial questions.

<span style="color:#34495E">**February 2027.**</span>
- *Discipline-building.* Convening event finalised — date, venue, format, invited participants. Target around twenty practitioners across the four domain groups (clinical, legal/regulated, research, creative/organisational). Two days, structured around the six anchor cases plus Miteo as a seventh.
- *Research PhD.* Thesis chapter scoping continues. First chapter draft begins. Co-supervisor engagement deepens.
- *Miteo.* Second Miteo working paper in early draft, building on the first.

<span style="color:#34495E">**March 2027.**</span>
- *Discipline-building.* Convening event held. Twenty practitioners attend; the event produces both community and documented critique of the pattern catalogue. Post-event: catalogue updates incorporating practitioner feedback; first informal expressions of interest in a future programme begin to surface.
- *Research PhD.* First thesis chapter draft complete. Confirmation review preparation begins (typically end of year one in Irish doctoral structure).
- *Miteo.* Continued substantive research progress. Working paper drafts accumulating.

### <span style="color:#16A085">Q3 and Q4 2027 (July–December) — First Cohort Possible</span>

By 2028, the founding-author phase is approaching its natural endpoint. The discipline has artefacts, practitioners, institutional standing, and at least one cohort of graduates. The work shifts from founding to scaling.

- *Wider institutional engagement.* Other institutions begin enquiring about adopting *Directing AI* materials, hosting their own cohorts, or partnering on faculty development. ATU's first-mover position becomes valuable as other institutions follow.

## <span style="color:#1B4F72">ATU strategy in detail</span>

ATU is the primary institutional partner. The strategy has three layers:

<span style="color:#34495E">**Layer 1: The Research PhD.**</span> This is the immediate operational ATU relationship. Application in Q3 2026. Admission decision Q4 2026. PhD work proceeds from early 2027. Todd is an ATU PhD candidate throughout the founding-author phase. The PhD itself is the institutional vehicle for foundational artefact authoring.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Layer 2: The programme conversation.**</span> Continues in parallel with the PhD, with the operating assumption that the conversation matures naturally once the discipline has visible substance. Initial conversation in Q3 or Q4 2026, framed as exploratory; substantive conversation in Q2 2027 once the pattern catalogue is published and the convening event has happened; formal proposal preparation in Q3 2027 if conversation is positive. The conversation is not a sales pitch; it is a continued thread that becomes a decision at the right moment.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Layer 3: The strategic position.**</span> ATU as the institution where the discipline forms, with Todd as its founding author and ATU PhD graduate. This is the long-term outcome the plan optimises for: ATU named as a discipline-defining institution because its own doctoral graduate authored the foundational work. The strategic position is built through Layer 1 and Layer 2 working together; it is not a separate action item.

The three layers are mutually reinforcing. The PhD gives the foundational work academic standing. The foundational work makes the programme conversation easy. The programme conversation gives the strategic position concrete form. None of the three requires ATU to make the larger strategic commitment the original Strategic Case document called for. Each is a small, achievable decision that adds up to the larger position incrementally.

## <span style="color:#1B4F72">Parallel institutional options</span>

ATU is primary. Other options continue in low-effort parallel:

<span style="color:#34495E">**Professional body endorsement.**</span> The Psychological Society of Ireland, the Law Society, the Marketing Institute, the various health-professional regulators. Initial outreach in Q1 2027 (after the pattern catalogue is published); aim for at least one CPD-endorsement conversation by Q3 2027. Professional-body endorsement is faster than university validation and produces real recognition for the credential when it launches.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Open University or UK partnership.**</span> Initial conversation in Q2 or Q3 2027, as a fallback option if ATU's programme conversation stalls. The Open University's interdisciplinary CPD work and several UK independent providers can credential at NFQ-equivalent levels in shorter timelines than traditional universities.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Foxxelabs as independent credentialer.**</span> Always available as a final fallback. The plan does not require this to be activated, but its availability gives the negotiating position with other institutions some weight: if no institutional partnership materialises by mid-2028, Foxxelabs can launch the credential independently and let market reception establish its value.

## <span style="color:#1B4F72">Decision points</span>

Five moments in the plan require explicit decision rather than continuous execution:

<span style="color:#34495E">**Q4 2026: PhD admission outcome.**</span> If accepted at ATU on a suitable topic, proceed as planned. If not accepted, immediate conversation with alternative institutions (Trinity, DCU, Maynooth, RCSI, Open University, UCL). The discipline-building work proceeds regardless; only the institutional vehicle changes.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Q2 2027: ATU programme conversation status.**</span> By end of June 2027, the conversation should have resolved to one of three states: formal proposal in preparation; informal continuation with no immediate proposal; not happening at ATU. The third outcome triggers active alternative-institution pursuit.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Q3 2027: First cohort launch decision.**</span> Based on which institutional vehicle is approved, decide whether autumn 2027 first cohort is feasible or whether it slips to 2028. The decision is shaped by approval timelines, recruitment readiness, and Todd's own bandwidth.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Q1 2028: Second-author identification.**</span> By early 2028, at least one early-cohort graduate or external practitioner should be identified as a developing second lecturer. Without this, cohorts three and four become unsustainable.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Q3 2028: Textbook publication strategy.**</span> Self-published online, open-access with an academic publisher, or traditional academic press? The decision depends on what offers have materialised by this point and on the discipline's profile at that moment.

## <span style="color:#1B4F72">Risks specific to this plan</span>

<span style="color:#34495E">**PhD topic scoping.**</span> The PhD topic must be substantial enough to be doctorally defensible and bounded enough to be completable. A topic framed as *"establishing Directing AI as a discipline"* is too broad. A topic framed as *"the empirical application of a documented Directing AI methodology to mitochondrial research"* with theoretical and methodological framing chapters is more defensible. Final scoping happens in supervisor conversation.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Supervisor capacity.**</span> ATU faculty supervising a Research PhD on AI orchestration methodology will be doing so in a forming discipline they have not themselves practised. This requires a supervisor willing to engage substantively with material they are learning alongside the candidate. The right supervisor is likely an interdisciplinary academic with broad methodological interests rather than a deep specialist in any particular component.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Bandwidth genuinely.**</span> The plan above assumes Miteo, PhD, discipline-building, and (potentially) first cohort teaching all happen in parallel through 2027. This is achievable only if the unifying-thread premise actually holds — if every hour of work genuinely produces outputs across multiple threads. If the threads pull apart in practice, one or more has to give, and the most likely casualties are second cohort timing and convening-event scaling.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Institutional drift.**</span> ATU's response to a Research PhD application is more predictable than its response to a discipline-defining programme proposal. But "more predictable" is not "certain." If PhD admission is delayed, the entire plan's early phases slow. If supervision goes wrong, the PhD itself becomes a drag rather than a vehicle.

<span style="color:#34495E">**Miteo's research outputs.**</span> Mitochondrial research is genuinely hard. The plan assumes Miteo produces publishable outputs at roughly the rate stated above. If the substantive research stalls (the data doesn't support the hypotheses; the literature turns out to contradict the framing; the questions turn out to be harder than estimated), Miteo's case-study value diminishes and the discipline-building work loses one of its most compelling artefacts.

## <span style="color:#1B4F72">What success looks like at end of 2028</span>

By December 2028, if the plan executes well, the following exists:

A published pattern catalogue v3 with community contributions integrated, recognised as the canonical reference for *Directing AI* patterns. A case library with twenty or more documented cases. A textbook v1 in some form of publication. Two Miteo papers in peer-reviewed journals plus several pre-prints. A Research PhD thesis substantially drafted (with completion in 2029 or 2030). Two or three cohorts of programme graduates entering practice. At least one second-generation lecturer developing into independent teaching capacity. ATU named as the institution where the discipline emerged, recognised through Todd's PhD candidacy, programme co-validation, and presence in the foundational artefacts. Other institutions enquiring about adopting the discipline, hosting cohorts, or partnering on faculty development.

Most importantly: the discipline exists as a recognised body of work that can be carried forward by others. The founding-author phase has completed not by exhausting Todd's contribution but by having produced enough that subsequent generations can build on it. That is the right outcome for this phase. The institutional consolidation — the *BSc (Honours) in Directing AI*, the formal departmental structure, the established PhD pipeline — happens in 2029, 2030, and beyond, under others' active leadership with Todd's continued advisory presence.

The discipline you're founding will outlive your active founding leadership. By end of 2028, it is set up to do so.
